Choose the correct option from the statement regarding Correlated and Non Correlated sub-queries. Which of the following statements is true about correlated and non-correlated sub-queries?


1.  Correlated sub-queries always execute before non-correlated sub-queries.

2.  Non-correlated sub-queries are more efficient than correlated sub-queries.

3.  Correlated sub-queries are dependent on the outer query while non-correlated sub-queries are independent of the outer query.

4.  Non-correlated sub-queries are always faster than correlated sub-queries.






Answer: 3.  Correlated sub-queries are dependent on the outer query while non-correlated sub-queries are independent of the outer query.
