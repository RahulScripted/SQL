SELECT order.id
    FROM orders AS sub
    WHERE price > (
        SELECT AVG(price)
        FROM orders
        WHERE category = sub.category
    );
What exactly does the above query output?


What of the following is the query trying to output?

1.  Order_id where the price of an item is greater than the average price for the item's category.

2.  Order_id where the price of an item is lesser than the average price for the item's category.	





Answer: 1.  Order_id where the price of an item is greater than the average price for the item's category.
